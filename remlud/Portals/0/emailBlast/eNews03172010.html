Are You On OSHA's List?

  
Recently, the federal Occupation Safety and Health Administration (OSHA) released a list of some 15,000 employers with the highest numbers of injuries and illnesses in the nation for their respective industry.  Under new directives from the Obama Administration, OSHA recently sent a letter to these employers placing them on notice and giving them information regarding their specific data.  Most likely the letter will not be the end of OSHA's enforcement actions.
 
Did you receive one of these OSHA letters?  Thanks to a Freedom of Information Act request, we are providing a database of Nebraska-based employers that did.  View the database of Nebraska-based employers [pdf]
