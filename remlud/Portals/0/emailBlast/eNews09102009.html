Reminder:  LB 403 Requirements Regarding Public Benefits, Employment, and Contracting Effective October 1, 2009

  
Municipalities and other Nebraska political subdivisions and state agencies must comply with the requirements of LB 403 in the dispensing of public benefits, hiring of employees, and public contracting starting October 1, 2009.  Download eNews [pdf].
