District Court Upholds Occupation Tax for Compact Compliance

  
In a decision issued March 12, 2010, the Lancaster County District Court, Judge Robert Otte, in the case Kiplinger v. Nebraska Department of Natural Resources et al., found that the occupation tax authorized by LB 701 to be levied by Republican River basin Natural Resources Districts (“NRDs”) to raise funds to aid in maintaining Republican River Compact compliance did not violate the Nebraska Constitution.
