Stohs Joins Rembolt Ludtke

  

Rembolt Ludtke LLP is pleased to announce that Brett C. Stohs has become associated with the firm. Stohs is a graduate of the University of Nebraska at Lincoln (Bachelor of Arts, with honors, 2001) and Duke University (Juris Doctor, cum laude, 2005).

Stohs practiced law in Washington, D.C. with a large international law firm for five years before returning to Nebraska. His practice is concentrated in the areas of business and corporate work, including contracts, entities, nonprofit organizations, and mergers and acquisitions.
