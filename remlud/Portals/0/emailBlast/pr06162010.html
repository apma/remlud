Rembolt Ludtke Announces Blog Site on U.S. Agriculture

 
We are pleased to announce the launch of a new blog site focused on the policies and regulations affecting agribusiness in the state of Nebraska and throughout the United States.  In her No Bull Manure: Survival of Agriculture blog, Rembolt Ludtke attorney Sarah Pillen provides a candid pro-producer view of the latest developments and government interplay in the U.S. agricultural sector.  This No Bull commentary, which is accessible at huskerscornandpork.blogspot.com, is an informative resource of interest to those working in the agricultural field and individuals seeking self-development.  Comments to the issues presented are always encouraged.

Sarah Pillen is an associate in the Lincoln, Nebraska office where she practices in labor and employment, corporate, real estate, and business transactions law with a particular emphasis on the agricultural industry.  She was raised in a livestock driven family business, which has played a significant role in shaping her practice.  Sarah serves as director of local community organizations such as the Lincoln Children's Museum, Lincoln Parks Foundation, Runners With A Reason, and A-FAN.  In 2007, she was a participant in the State Chamber's Leadership Nebraska Program.
