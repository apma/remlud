Attorney David Bargen Offers Expertise at Water Law Conference

  

The University of Nebraska College of Law and University of Nebraska-Lincoln Water Center held its annual one-day water law conference on October 6, 2010.  The event brought together practicing attorneys specializing in water law and other business and government professionals from the Great Plains region.  Municipal and water law attorney David Bargen served on the conference planning committee and also presented information at the conference on the topic of boundary disputes involving land bordering rivers and streams. 

Materials from Mr. Bargen's segment at the conference on riparian boundary disputes are available now for download from our website. Follow this link to access the materials:  http://www.remboltludtke.com/adefault.asp?page=legalnews&suba=scurrent&m=seminar.  If you are interested in learning more, or have general questions about water law, please contact Mr. Bargen at 402.475.5100 or email him at dbargen@remboltludtke.com.
