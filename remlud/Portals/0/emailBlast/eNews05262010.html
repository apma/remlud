The 8th Circuit Provides Employers With Guidance on Avoiding Retaliation Claims

  

The Eighth Circuit Court of Appeals recently issued an opinion providing employers with an excellent roadmap to avoid liability for retaliation claims asserted by employees terminated for performance reasons.
