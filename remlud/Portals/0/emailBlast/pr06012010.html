Rembolt Ludtke Launches Blog Covering Water Law Topics

  

Water Watch, the firm’s latest blog site, contains posts and links to news about water issues affecting Nebraska and the wider Great Plains region, contributed by Water and Municipal Law attorney David Bargen.  In the blog, David covers recent developments in this ever-changing area of law.  We invite you to visit the site which is accessible at remboltludtke.blogspot.com.  As always, we encourage your feedback on the topics presented.

David Bargen is an associate in the Lincoln, Nebraska office practicing in municipal and water law, as well as general litigation.  David clerked for the Hon. C. Arlen Beam on the United States Court of Appeals for the Eighth Circuit for two years prior to joining the firm.  In 2009, David was appointed to an advisory board for a groundwater awareness program sponsored by the Groundwater Foundation and funded by the Nebraska Department of Environmental Quality.  In 2008, David was a participant in the State Chamber’s Leadership Nebraska program.
