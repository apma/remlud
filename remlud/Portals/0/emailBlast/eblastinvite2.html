Business Planning in Challenging Times

For those concerned with how to effectively manage a business in the current economic climate, please join us for a practical, two-hour seminar. The keynote speaker will be the Director of the Nebraska Department of Economic Development, speaking on the Nebraska Advantage Act. Also joining this event will be Nebraska head basketball coach Doc Sadler, focusing on leadership development.

The Nebraska Advantage Act can provide significant tax credits for businesses which expand their operations in the state. An overview of the Act can be found in our September 2008 newsletter [pdf], The Compass.

The seminar is scheduled for the afternoon before the Spring Game. Registration is free; space is limited, so reply today.
